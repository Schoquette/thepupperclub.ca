The Nitty Gritty

We don't believe in a set structure, as each pup has unique needs. Those may even vary on a day to day basis. That's why we call them visits and not simply dog walks.

Curating means a white-glove treatment for you and your dog, and that includes anything we can fit in the time window. During our meet and greet, we will collect information from you on our starting point for how to plan the visits.

Some examples of tailoring are:

  • Solo or paired walks if your pup would benefit from socialization
  • Sniff-focused, enrichment-led routes for exercising the brain and less mobile pups
  • On-leash, low traffic routes, or off-leash and dog park visits
  • Indoor play on rainy days or for low-activity dogs
  • Paw wipes and post-walk refresh
  • Feeding and water refresh
  • Light grooming touches, including bathing, coat smoothing and nail trimming using a nail grinder
  • Medication administration and health screening
  • Reinforcement of training
  • Scheduled trips to the groomer or vet upon request
  • Minor home maintenance such as watering plants or feeding other pets

All visits include daily photos + report and Tractive GPS tracking during visits.

Depending on availability, other services are available at an additional charge. These include:

  • In-home dog-sitting
  • Staff-home dog-sitting
  • Extended visits (for example, taking a dog to multiple appointments)
  • Weekend and holiday visits

If none of these packages work for you, or you have a special request, please reach out. We are happy to try to accommodate your needs.

Our packages are based on weekday visits, and exclude statutory holidays.

Our Process

01

Set up a meet and greet

We will visit your home to meet your pup and learn more about you, and them. Our intake process is thorough, so that we have all the info we need to safely look after your pup, and give them the best time.

02

Housekeeping

We will have some paperwork to sign and policies to review. We use a platform called TimeToPet to manage your information, scheduling, and invoicing. We'll coordinate access to your home using a spare key or lockbox that we can provide.

03

Get started

We will get scheduling and walking! We will (probably over) communicate with you to make sure you are in the loop on our visits, and feel confident that your pup is getting the ideal care for them.

04

Always improve

We will check in regularly to see if there is anything we need to tweak in the care plan, or scheduling. We are always looking for ways to improve each individual experience, as well as how we operate as a business.
