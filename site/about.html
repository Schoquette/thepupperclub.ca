Who we are

Hi, I'm Sophie — the founder of The Pupper Club.

My professional background is in operations, across the hospitality, high-tech, and legal industries. I've spent years building systems that make experiences feel smooth and consistent — and I bring that same mindset to dog care.

I've always been brought back to dogs. I've had them my whole life, and I've also been volunteering in dog rescue for a decade. Dogs have a way of keeping me grounded — and they deserve care that's thoughtful, steady, and tailored to who they are.

The Pupper Club started with a simple question: How would I want my own dog to be cared for? For my dog, Maya, routine and continuity matter. She does best when her day feels predictable, calm, and supported — not rushed. When I had friends look after her that smothered her in kisses and I could tell truly felt like it was gift to look after her, it was game-changer.

That's what I aim to provide for every dog in my care: unrushed visits, consistent handling, and a routine that helps them feel safe and fulfilled — and helps you feel confident.

The Pupper Club is insured, registered, and all handlers receive Red Cross pet first aid training.
